A 402m drag race, commonly known as a quarter-mile drag race, is a motorsport event where two vehicles compete to cover a straight distance of 402 meters (approximately 1,320 feet) in the shortest time possible. These races are typically held on a drag strip and focus on the vehicles' acceleration, speed, and driver skill, providing a thrilling and competitive spectacle.
